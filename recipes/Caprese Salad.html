Caprese Salad

Description:

Caprese salad is a simple and refreshing Italian salad that showcases the flavors of ripe tomatoes, fresh mozzarella cheese, and fragrant basil leaves. It is named after the island of Capri in Italy and is a popular dish in Italian cuisine. The salad is typically composed of thick slices of juicy tomatoes, slices of creamy mozzarella cheese, and whole basil leaves arranged in a visually appealing manner. The salad is then drizzled with extra-virgin olive oil, sprinkled with a pinch of salt, and sometimes dressed with a splash of balsamic vinegar. Caprese salad is a celebration of fresh and vibrant ingredients, highlighting the natural sweetness of tomatoes, the smoothness of mozzarella, and the aromatic essence of basil. It is a light and delightful appetizer or side dish that captures the essence of Mediterranean flavors.

Ingredients

  • Ripe tomatoes
  • Fresh mozzarella cheese
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Garlic
  • Extra-virgin olive oil
  • Salt
