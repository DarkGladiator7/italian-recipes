Lasagna

Description:

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish known for its layers of pasta sheets, rich tomato sauce, cheese, and various fillings. It is a baked casserole that combines flavors and textures to create a hearty and satisfying meal. Traditionally, lasagna is made with alternating layers of pasta, meat sauce, and cheese, typically mozzarella and ricotta. However, there are countless variations available, including vegetarian and vegan options. The dish is often seasoned with herbs like oregano and basil, giving it a distinct Italian flavor. Once assembled, lasagna is baked in the oven until the pasta is tender and the cheese is melted and golden brown. It is a beloved comfort food enjoyed by people around the world.

Ingredients

  • Ground meat
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Tomato Paste
  • Italian Seasoning
  • Salt and pepper
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese

  • Fresh Herbs
